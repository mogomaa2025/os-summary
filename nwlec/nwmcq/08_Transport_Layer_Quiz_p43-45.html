Explanation

Which of the following applications would be a typical use case for UDP?
Explanation
The slide lists "Voice Streaming" and "Video Streaming" as use cases for UDP, where speed is more important than guaranteed delivery of every single packet. E-mail and file sharing require the reliability of TCP.
